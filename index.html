Message from Vice-Chancellor

Welcome to North South University (NSU), and it is indeed a great pleasure for me to address you here. Established in 1992 as the first private university in Bangladesh, NSU has grown into a leading center of excellence in higher education, known for its academic programs and contributions to research and community development. Today this center, our university, has earned the trust of people, both nationally and internationally.
